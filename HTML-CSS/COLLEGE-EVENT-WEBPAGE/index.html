About CodeFusion 2026

CodeFusion is the annual technical festival of LCIT that brings together students passionate about technology, innovation, and problem-solving. The event provides opportunities to learn from experts, participate in competitions, and collaborate with peers.

  • Coding Compitition
  • Hackathon
  • Web Development Workshop
  • AI & Machine Learning Session
  • Robotics Exhibition
  • Technical Quiz
